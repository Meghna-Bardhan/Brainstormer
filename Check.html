Rules

  • 1. Students of class IX to XII of any Board and Undergraduate students,pursuing their Bachelor degree ONLY are allowed to participate.
  • 2. Pass-outs that is students who have already completed their graduation are strictly NOT allowed to participate.
  • 3. Please make sure you bring your Identity Card (School ID/College ID/Library Card) on the day of the event.
  • 4. On-Spot registration will also be there,but it is a subject to crowd and team space availability.So Teams are advised to register prior to the event in the website.
  • 5. Teams can comprise of students from different institutions(school/college).

About Us

Phoenix is the official tech-club of Netaji Subhash Engineering College. It was established in 2006 under the able leadership of a few seniors of the college. Over the years, Phoenix has grown to be the tech - hub of NSEC with a membership of over 200 students from various streams.
